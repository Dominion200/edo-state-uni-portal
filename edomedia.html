Edo State University

at EDO STATE UNIVERISTY, we believe that innovation is not just about technology. We see innovation as doing things differently to achieve a better result. This has led to the birth of The Ubemilin Centre for Tomorrow (Edo state university Innovation hub). The term Ubemilin is coined from the local Benin dialect meaning ‘to nurture’. The Ubemilin Centre for Tomorrow is Wellspring University’s intervention vehicle to nurture, manage and promote innovative thinking and initiatives. Here, we would birth social impact enterprises, entrepreneurial ideas, promote advocacy and leadership initiatives beyond the classroom.
